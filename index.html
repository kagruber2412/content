Hello!

I'm assistant professor in Econometrics at Erasmus University Rotterdam. My fields of expertise are quantitative marketing, psychometric methods and computational statistics.

In my research I mainly focus on Bayesian approximate methods for individual-level inference in large-scale problems. I'm currently also working on methods for regularized inference in chain graphs.

Research Interests

  • Bayesian computing
  • Multivariate analysis
  • Categorical data analysis
